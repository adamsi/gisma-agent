Fruits Knowledge Base

1. **Solarberry**
   - Color: Golden Orange
   - Taste: Sweet with a hint of citrus
   - Origin: High-altitude regions of Solara Valley
   - Description: Solarberries glow faintly under sunlight due to their high beta-carotene content. They are often used in solar jams and radiant smoothies.

2. **Moonapple**
   - Color: Pale silver-white
   - Taste: Mildly tart with vanilla undertones
   - Origin: Grown only at night in the cold climates of Lunara Ridge
   - Description: Known for its luminous peel and sleep-inducing aroma. The Moonapple is believed to promote lucid dreaming.

3. **Crimsonpine**
   - Color: Deep red
   - Taste: Sharp and tangy, similar to pineapple and cranberry
   - Origin: Forests of Ardentia
   - Description: A spiky-skinned fruit that grows on conifer-like trees. Its juice is often used in fiery cocktails and stamina tonics.

5. **Cloudmelon**
   - Color: Misty white with a blue hue
